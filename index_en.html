Short Bio

During my Bachelor and Master's studies I've taken part on several project concentrated around numerical methods and optimization of scientific code. I have worked as research assistant on Brno University of technology in an interdisciplinary project targeted on Ultrasound Transmission Tomography. For my Master's thesis I collaborated on design and implementation of automated methods for optimization of finite element method assembly subroutines. This project arose from simulation of haptical interaction with soft tissues, which I helped with as I joined the research group.
